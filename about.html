The Payana Story

The idea of a ‘Decentralized Travel Marketplace’ was conceived by our extremely proficient team comprising of Mr. Anup Pai, eSamudaay, Mr. Saneejt Rao, O2U2 Systems & Solutions, and Mr. Murali HR, Travel Resource Innovation Group, who’ve worked on multiple travel projects before. The idea was to come up with a travel marketplace that was led by travelers and the local travel hustlers, free from any middlemen. A sector in the tourism and travel industry that was waiting to be explored. This concept was much-needed to not only transform travel expeditions but also aid in promoting and encouraging the authentic culture of a place.

The travel industry has its own set of seasoned professionals: Priceline and Expedia in hospitality; Amadeus, Sabre, and Travelport in air travel; AirBnB and VRBO in vacation rentals.

While these companies provide tremendous value the world over, they also have a high transaction cost (15% average in hospitality, and sometimes even more in other fields). The real reason why according to some experts, travel is 10 years behind in terms of the adoption of new technologies, is because those professionals make sure the entry barriers are high while lacking incentives to innovate.

The Challenge

After experimenting with multiple approaches, we finally decided that Payana will have 3 key features: Discovery, Trusting & Authentic Experiences, and End-to-End Curation of the travel journey. Which could only be achieved by creating a travel marketplace, giving birth to ‘Payana’.

The Solution

Enabling and empowering local travel hustlers by promoting and encouraging authentic, cognitive, and experience-based native trips. A win-win for them and for the travelers.

How do we work with LENs?

Payana provides a secure and strong platform for local travel hustlers by keeping them connected and forming a community.
LENs will be able to enhance travelers' experience by providing them with the right resources through Payana.
LENs will be able to grow and expand their business by designing unique tours and experiences, enabling responsible travel, creating uniform products and services, and more!

Become a Destination’s ‘Hero’!

Payana leverages special aid to the travel LENs or local travel hustlers to create an authentic and alluring brand in the destination, to promote the destinations’ DNA. Furthermore, launching aspirational campaigns that aim to inspire and engage the tourist with enchanting experiences.

Offer Unique Products & Services

Payana will help the DAOs create appropriate products and services after identifying the destinations’ DNA. This will enable us to create unique, innovative products, experiences, and services with LENs for attracting tourists to the destination.

Design Inviting Tours & Experiences

Payana will help the DAOs design tours & experiences by understanding the customer journey map, identifying touchpoints, while designing services around the touchpoints. LENs will be able to measure the quality and impact of the products and services with the help of Payana’s specific design methods and tools
